About Us

Welcome to Sathitpathum Demonstration School, a beacon of excellence in education and innovation. As a leading educational institution, we are committed to nurturing young minds and empowering future generations. With a rich heritage spanning decades, our school has consistently embraced a holistic approach to education, fostering academic excellence, character development, and a love for lifelong learning. Our dedicated faculty and staff strive to create a dynamic and inclusive learning environment that inspires creativity, critical thinking, and global awareness. At Sathitpathum Demonstration School, we are driven by our passion to equip our students with the skills, knowledge, and values needed to thrive in a rapidly evolving world. Join us on this transformative journey where every child can unlock their full potential and become the leaders of tomorrow.

History

Prep International School was founded in 1996 on what was previously a private house and property in Huay Kwang, Bangkok. Prep originally called PEP began with small roll students but has grown to an enrollment of about 50 students spanning from Nursery to Kindergarten 3.

The campus has a warm, at-home feel and we at Prep like to think of it as our home away from home. We have a Montessori room, a grassy space for the children to play. On with plenty of playground equipment. Mature trees provide a restful setting. We have only 50 seats for children enrolled in our Nursery and Kindergarten classes.

The Kindergarten operates year-round, with teaching periods divided into 4 quarters that Prep welcomes parent of prospective students to visit us by walk-in or appointment since then two more schools have opened in Bangkok and the surrounding area, the latest one Prep International School in Pathumthani where we offer the Australian curriculum from Nursery to Kindergarten. This is also where we opened the English Program under Sathit Pathum Demonstration School ‚ÄîPrep for Interkids and was later known as Intensive Learning Program or ILP up to this day.

The focus od early childhood education is to provide a supportive environment that enables children to grow and develop into responsible, healthy citizens and effective life long learners.
